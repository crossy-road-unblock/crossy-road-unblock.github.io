🕹️ What is Crossy Road?

Crossy Road is an arcade-style game created by Hipster Whale. It was originally released on November 20, 2014. The goal is simple but addictive: guide your character as far as possible by avoiding obstacles like traffic, rivers, and trains. It features charming retro-style graphics and a wide range of unlockable characters, from chickens to unicorns and even robots.

👨‍💻 Who Made Crossy Road?

The game was developed by Hipster Whale, an Australian game studio founded by Andy Sum, Matt Hall, and Ben Weatherall.
